About Us

SCOM (Student Christian Organisation Of Malawi) is an indigenous interdenominational Christian Organisation committed to evangelism, discipleship and training of students so that they would love and serve the Lord effectively in the Church and the world.

MUBAS SCOM Evening meets every Wednesday from 17:45 pm to 20:30 pm in the Main Lecture Theatre.

We also have SCOM Lunch Hour every Wednesday during lunch times, where the venue is communicated in the WhatsApp MUBAS SCOM group.
