About Us

SCOM (Student Christian Organisation of Malawi) is an interdenominational Christian organisation committed to evangelism, discipleship, and training students to serve the Lord effectively.

MUBAS SCOM Evening meets every Wednesday from 17:45 pm to 20:30 pm in the Main Lecture Theatre.

We also have SCOM Lunch Hour every Wednesday during lunch times, with the venue communicated in the WhatsApp MUBAS SCOM group.
